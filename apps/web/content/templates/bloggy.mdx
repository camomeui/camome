## Highlights

- Based on [Astro Paper 2.0](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper) with modified design.
- Styled by Camome UI instead of Tailwind.
- Type-safe markdown.
- SEO-friendly.
- Light & dark mode.
- Fuzzy search.
- Draft posts & pagination.
- Sitemap & RSS feed.

## Tech stacks

- Astro
- Camome UI
- CSS Modules, Sass
